Lecture Courses

V. N. Karazin Kharkov National University

Quantum Chemistry 2008 - 2015

Course for the students of the school of Radiophysics of Karazin Kharkov national University.

Focuses on the basics of Quantum Mechanics and Quantum Chemistry for understanding of the main principles of the modern computational chemistry methods.

Practical tasks during the classes helps to learn how to choose appropriate method for the particular tasks and how to interpret results of calculations.

Cheminformatics for the drug discovery 2013 - 2015

Course for the students of the school of Chemistry (specialisation "Pharmaceutical Chemistry")

Describes, helps to understand, use properly and develop:

  • storing and managing chemical data
  • molecular descriptors and QSPR models
  • molecular docking and virtual screening tools

Physical methods in Chemistry 2010 - 2012

Course for the students of the Chemical department, describes main principles of the various spectroscopy methods on the base of understanding how matter interacts with the radiation.

  • basic principles of radiation-matter interaction and spectroscopy
  • mass-spectrometry
  • Vibrational spectroscopy
  • Infrared and Raman spectroscopy
  • Electronic (UV/VIS, fluorescence) spectroscopy
  • NMR and ESR methods
  • Moessbauer spectroscopy

Bialystok University of Technology

Selected mathematical methods in data science 2016 - present

Course for the postgraduate students of the 'Data Science' direction at Bialystok University of Technology.

Focuses on the basics linear algebra, methods of optimization and numerical methods.

Practical tasks during the classes helps to understand use of these approaches within NumPy Python library.
